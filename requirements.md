## Notes
- "Frutiger Aero" aesthetic relies heavily on glassmorphism (backdrop-filter), glossy gradients, and vibrant colors (Sky Blue, Grass Green).
- The interface mimics a Desktop OS (Windows/Mac hybrid).
- Icons will use Lucide-React but styled to look more skeuomorphic with backgrounds.
- Music player will use a standard HTML5 audio element with custom controls.
